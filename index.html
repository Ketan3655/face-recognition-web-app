Face Recognition System

A Face Recognition Based Attendance System is a modern and efficient solution for schools, colleges, coaching classes, and training centers to track student attendance. This system utilizes advanced facial recognition technology to identify and confirm the attendance of students. By simply taking a single photo, the system generates an attendance report instantly, providing a quick and efficient way to record attendance. .
